Brazilian Lemonade

Description

This recipe is a must-have for the summer months when it's scorching hot. Not only is this a refreshing drink, but it's one that begs people to have another glass. It is a great party drink for lounging out by the pool, the lake, or a family bbq.

Ingredients

  • 8 juicy limes
  • 2 tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 4 quarts of water

Steps

  1. Cut the limes in half and juice them.
  2. Add the juice, water, and residual lime halves into a blender.
  3. Use the pulse feature on the blender to achieve a cloudy concoction. You should not have to pulse more than 8 times.
  4. Strain the concoction and discard the strained pulp and lime halves.
  5. Return to strained liquid back into the blender.
  6. Add the condensed milk to the liquid.
  7. Blend the mixture until incorporated.
  8. Serve in a glass with ice. Serve with a lime wedge for garnish. (Optional)
  9. Enjoy!
