Tuna Melt

Description

This recipe is an absolute game changer. It maximizes the deliciousness to prep time ratio and is consistently in the rotation within the household. It's a tried and true fan favorite.

In addition, this meal shouldn't break the bank and can be prepared with ingredients for <$10 dollars!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz chunk light tuna. Tuna in oil or water is okay.
  • 4 oz of mayonnaise
  • 1 stalk of celery. Finely cubed
  • Fresh ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 slice of Kraft cheese
  • Your choice of sliced bread. We like 7 seed.
  • 1 tbsp finely diced Gherkin pickle
  • 2 oz pickle juice

Steps

  1. Add the chunk light tuna, mayonnaise, diced celery, diced pickles, and pickle juice to a bowl. Using a fork, mash and combine the ingredients.
  2. Add the salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Scoop the desired amount of the tuna preparation onto a slice of bread.
  4. Layer the slice of cheese onto the tuna.
  5. Add the remaining slice of bread to create a sandwich.
  6. Lightly toast the sandwich on either side using a skillet on medium heat. Be sure to check the bread consistently to prevent burning.
  7. Enjoy with a side salad, bag of chips, or by itself!
